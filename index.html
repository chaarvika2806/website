Project: 1

Construction of Flexible Pavements and Culverts

    Construction process:
  • In the process of laying the road, the path has a drainage passage which cannot be blocked. So a slab culvert had been designed for it.
  • It works as the road on the top and there is an opening beneath the slab from where the drainage water can flow. Abutments are built with seepage pipes.
  • It helps drainage water to flow from one side to the other side inside the abutments and protect the material inside the abutments and it's strength.

    My experience:

Project: 2

Designing, Modelling and energy analysis of residential buildings using BIM

Introduction:

Recently im working on an interesting project in the department of Construction Engineering and Management with a team of 3 people. Our project is about designing, modelling, and energy analysis of a residential building using BIM. It is interesting as well as challenging topic because BIM tool is not used much in India due to the lack of awareness of its advantages in real life applications.

Scope:

The main aim of doing this project is to analyse the energy efficiency of the building even before it is built using suitable software utilising natural energy properly and reduce the usage of electricity and other artificial sources of conditioning.

Methodology:

    Since there is no existing building which is constructed or analyzed using BIM for energy efficiency in India
  • We designed the plan of a residential building in AUTOCAD
  • Prepared a 3D model in Revit software.
  • We provided the materials for different parts of the building which are commonly used in India.
  • Also we provided different types of ventilation in the building and solar panels on the terrace.
  • We learned two new softwares Insight, Design Builders for the energy efficiency analysis process.
  • Now we are planning to give four locations from all over India.

Conclusion:

Run the energy analysis to record the differences the differences in readings and the accuracy we have obtained by using BIM tool for this project.
